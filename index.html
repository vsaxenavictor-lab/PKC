⚖️ Tax Year / AY Reference
Under IT Act 1961: Assessment Year (AY) follows the Previous Year (PY). e.g. PY 2024-25 → AY 2025-26.
Under IT Act 2025 (effective from Tax Year 2026-27 onwards): "Tax Year" replaces "Previous Year". AY dropdowns will show Tax Year labels for AY 2026-27 and later. The system automatically caps AY to the current financial year.
